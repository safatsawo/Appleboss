    Location

    Menaggio, Lombardy, Italy

    A beautiful leafy neighbourhood on the shores of Lake Como, within walking distance of the centre of Menaggio, with great stores, restaurants and a lido. This is also the departure point for ferries to Bellagio, Varenna, Tremezzo and Como.
